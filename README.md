# AXIOM: The Visual Calculus Engine

> **Bridge the gap between abstract integration and geometric intuition.**

AXIOM is a high-fidelity, interactive educational simulator designed to visualize the fundamental principles of calculus. By decomposing complex manifolds into intuitive geometric primitives, AXIOM allows students to "see" the synthesis of area and volume in real-time.

## 🔬 Core Exploration Modules

*   **Riemannian Synthesis:** Observe the convergence of discrete sums into continuous area under arbitrary curves.
*   **Geometric Deconstruction:** Derivation of circle area through concentric ring integration.
*   **Volumetric Manifolds:** 3D synthesis of sphere volume using the "Method of Disks."
*   **Paradoxical Geometry (Gabriel's Horn):** Interactive exploration of solids with finite volume but infinite surface area.
*   **The FTC Bridge:** A dual-pane visualization of the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus (Rate-of-Change vs. Accumulation).

## ⚡ Engineering & Performance

*   **Zero-Latency Interactions:** Optimized SVG rendering engine for smooth 60FPS parameter manipulation.
*   **Mathematical Precision:** High-accuracy numerical integration tailored for educational accuracy.
*   **Academic Typography:** Publication-quality mathematical notation via KaTeX.
*   **Type-Safe Architecture:** Built with React and TypeScript for maximum reliability and maintainability.
